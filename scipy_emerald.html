Symbolic solution and visualization of ODEs (e.g., harmonic oscillator). Initial parameters from Google (.csv), symbolic execution in Jupyter, model serialization in .pkl.

  • Initial Conditions: ode_initial_conditions.csv
  • Serialized Model: ode_model.pkl
